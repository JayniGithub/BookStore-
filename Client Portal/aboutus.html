ABOUT US

...We Believe that Books can Change Your Life...
MISSION

Our mission is to give people around the world who lack access to books the opportunity to read for pleasure, study and lifelong learning.

VISION

Our vision is a world where everyone has access to books that will enrich, improve and change their lives.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

Give people power.

They inform and inspire, and help people create a better, more equal future.

Should experience the power of books.

Everybody everywhere should have access to books, whatever their circumstances.

Partnerships are vital.

By working together we can create sustainable change and give more people the opportunity to read.
